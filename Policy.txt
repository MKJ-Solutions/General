Company Name: MKJ Solutions
Company Members: Miles, Johnson, Kyong-Tae

----------Definition----------
closed - no further updates, additions or changes can be made to a project.
completion date - a date, agreed upon all members, to complete a project.
----------Company Policy----------
1.When a project is not done within the completion date, project is closed permanently.
2.When a project is completed within the completion date, company members shall go have a dinner
  together within the week of the completion date.
